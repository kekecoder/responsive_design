Tapenade (Olive Spread)

This ia a really simple dish to prepare and it's always a big hit at parties. My father recommends:

Make this the night before so that the flavors have time to blend it up to room temperature before you serve it. In the winter, try serving it warm.

Ingredients

  • 1 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2/3 c. kalamata olives
  • 1 t. capers

Instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes and garlic in a food processor. Blend until as smooth as possible

  2. Add capers and olives. Pulse the motor a few times unti they are incorporated, but still retain some texture

  3. Serve on thin toast rounds with goat and fresh basil garnish (optional)
