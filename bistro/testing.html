linotype
line casting allowed type to be selected, used, then recirculated into the machine automatically. Yhis advance increased the speed of typesetting and printing dramatically.
Photocomposition
Typefaces are stored on film then projected onto photo-sensitive paper. Lenses adjust the size of the type.
Digital Type

Digital typefaces store the outline of the font shape in a format such as Postscript. The outline maybe scaled to any size for output.

Postscript emerged as a standard due to its support of graphics and its early support on Macintosh computer and Apple laser printer.
